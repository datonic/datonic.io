Datonic

Datonic is, for now, only an idea.

It aims to be the best way to share and collaborate on open datasets. It is not a new standard. It aims to build upon, coalesce, and make bridges.

Our goal is to bring Open Data to the level of Open Source by making Open Data compatible with the modern data ecosystem.

Ideals to Strive

  • Community Focus
  • Modularity
  • Permissionless Ecosystem
  • Declarative Tooling
  • Inmutability
  • Serverless Approach
  • Decentralized
  • Reproducible and Accessible
  • Optimized for Humans
